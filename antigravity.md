# ANTIGRAVITY.md - Eye of Horus Vision

## 1. The Mission
To act as the **All-Seeing Guardian** for digital agencies. We transform "passive monitoring" into "proactive strategy." Eye of Horus ensures that no technical glitch or marketing dip goes unnoticed, protecting the client’s ROI and the agency’s reputation.

## 2. The Weight (Gravity we are removing)
- **Manual Reporting:** Hours wasted compiling GA4/GSC data into PDFs.
- **Reactive Panic:** Discovering a website form is broken only after a client complains.
- **Data Fragmentation:** Having data in five different tabs (Clarity, Search Console, WP-Admin, etc.).
- **Short-termism:** Losing sight of 18-month trends due to focusing only on the "now."

## 3. The Lift (How we soar)
- **Historical Wisdom:** By maintaining 18 months of context, we provide GA4/Data Studio-level depth, showing YoY growth that proves agency value.
- **The AI Strategist:** We don't just report that "traffic is down"; the AI uses historical data and technical logs to suggest *why* and how to fix it.
- **Visual Command:** A dashboard that feels like a premium "War Room"—Gold, Obsidian, and high-clarity data.
- **Autonomous QA:** The "Daily Scout" (Playwright) acts as a 24/7 QA team, testing forms and layouts across all devices before the workday even begins.

## 4. The "Horus" Roadmap
- **Awareness:** Real-time uptime, technical updates, and WhatsApp alerts.
- **Insight:** 18-month comparative analytics and SEO reporting.
- **Action:** AI-generated blog posts, competitor research, and automated marketing strategy briefs.

## 5. The Core Ethos
If a small issue becomes a client problem, the Eye of Horus has failed. We exist to ensure the agency always acts **before** the client notices. Every report, every alert, and every AI insight must be focused on one goal: **Getting more leads for the client.**
